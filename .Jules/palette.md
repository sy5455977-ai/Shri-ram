# Palette's Journal

## 2025-05-15 - Improving Accessibility and Focus States
**Learning:** Many icon-only buttons in the NEXUS AI interface rely solely on the `title` attribute or have no labeling at all, which is insufficient for screen readers. Additionally, focus states are often missing or subtle, making keyboard navigation difficult.
**Action:** Implement `aria-label` for all icon-only buttons and add distinct `focus-visible` ring styles to all interactive elements to ensure WCAG compliance and a better UX for all users.
